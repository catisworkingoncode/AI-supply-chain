# AI-supply-chain 
AI x Supply Chain analysis \
purpose: practice EDA + business analysis \ 


source from: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/abdulmaliklodhra/global-ai-chip-supply-chain-dataset-19902036/code?datasetId=9942918&sortBy=dateRun&tab=profile&excludeNonAccessedDatasources=false

Chart Description
1. Demand vs Production \
Question: Are we making enough chips to meet AI demand in each snapshot year? \
2. Historical Risk \
Which regions have been structurally fragile — and are getting better or worse? \
3. Geo Risk × Trade Dependency \
Who is both sanctioned AND heavily dependent on imports? (double-exposed) \
4. Regional Superstar Score \
Composite score: market share + AI production + low risk → who wins in 2036? \
5. Revenue vs GPU Demand \
Is the market monetizing AI hardware demand effectively over time? \
6. R&D vs Performance \
Is chip spending actually translating to better AI performance? \
7. Trade Balance \
Who are the net suppliers vs net importers, and is that shifting? \
8. Market Dashboard \
Macro view of global revenue, growth cycles, and price inflation \
9. Correlation Heatmap \
Hidden cross-dataset relationships at the 2036 midpoint \
10. Risk Decomposition \
For top producers, which specific risk types are growing 2026 -> 2036? \
